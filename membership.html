The Awesome Inc U Membership Program is like a gym fitness club for programmers. The best way to learn and excel at any skill is to surround yourself with other like-minded, motivated people. Members of Awesome Inc U will receive access to the Awesome Inc Space every Monday - Thursday evening from 5-7pm to hone their programming skills alongside other programmers. On Mondays and Wednesdays from 5-7pm, programming experts will be onsite to assist and coach Members through programming projects from curriculum we provide or programming projects of their own.

The standard rate for the Awesome Inc U Membership Program is $100 / month, however we're offering an introductory rate of $60 / month.
